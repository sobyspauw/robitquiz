## Board Games

### Backgammon
**Q:** How many checkers does each player start with?
**A:** 15 ✓ | 12 | 20 | 24
**Explanation:** Each player starts with 15 checkers in backgammon, placed in specific starting positions.

**Q:** How many points are on a backgammon board?
**A:** 24 ✓ | 32 | 18 | 26
**Explanation:** A backgammon board has 24 points (triangles), divided into four quadrants of 6 points each.

**Q:** What is the bar in backgammon?
**A:** Center divider for hit pieces ✓ | Scoring area | Starting position | Doubling cube location
**Explanation:** The bar is the center divider. When a checker is hit, it goes on the bar and must re-enter.

### Chess
**Q:** How many squares are on a chessboard?
**A:** 64 ✓ | 32 | 81 | 100
**Explanation:** A chessboard has 64 squares (8x8 grid), alternating light and dark colors.

**Q:** Which piece can only move diagonally?
**A:** Bishop ✓ | Rook | Knight | Queen
**Explanation:** The bishop moves diagonally any number of squares, staying on its original color throughout the game.

**Q:** What is castling?
**A:** Special king and rook move ✓ | Pawn promotion | Capturing en passant | Checkmating the king
**Explanation:** Castling involves moving the king 2 squares toward a rook, then moving that rook to the square the king crossed.

**Q:** How many pieces does each player start with?
**A:** 16 ✓ | 12 | 20 | 32
**Explanation:** Each player starts with 16 pieces: 1 king, 1 queen, 2 rooks, 2 bishops, 2 knights, and 8 pawns.

**Q:** What is en passant?
**A:** Special pawn capture ✓ | King's escape move | Queen's power move | Knight's jump
**Explanation:** En passant is a special pawn capture that occurs when an enemy pawn moves 2 squares forward from its starting position.

### Monopoly

**Q:** How much do you collect when passing GO?

**Q:** What color are the most expensive properties?

### Scrabble
**Q:** How many letter tiles are in standard Scrabble?
**A:** 100 ✓ | 98 | 102 | 96
**Explanation:** Standard Scrabble contains 100 letter tiles, including 2 blank tiles that can represent any letter.

**Q:** How many tiles does each player start with?
**A:** 7 ✓ | 5 | 10 | 8
**Explanation:** Each player starts with 7 tiles, drawn randomly from the bag, and replenishes after each turn.

**Q:** How much is the letter Z worth?

**Explanation:** Z is worth 10 points in Scrabble, tied with Q as the highest-value letters.
